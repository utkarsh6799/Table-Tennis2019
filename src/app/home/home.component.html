01

About Us

The 23rd annual Ajay Kumar Garg Memorial Table Tennis Tournament is to be conducted on 5-6 November 2019 at Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College in association with GATT, Affiliated with UPTTA. This tournament is state level which is held every year for all professional institutions and schools of the state. Various teams mark their participation in 10 different categories for men and women separately in individual and team events. A huge amount is kept as the prize money.

02

Eligibility

ENTRY FOR TEAM CHAMPIONSHIPS OPEN TO :
  • All professional Institutes Like Medical, Engineering, Management, B.Ed., Computer Applications etc.
  • Any Graduate/Post Graduate College.
  • Organizations (College/Schools).
  • All Boys and Girls Schools.
  • Entry for Individual events opens to Men & Women team members and their extra players.
  • Individual entries are accepted for individual events. Age rules will be followed as per norms of TTFI/UPTTA.
  • All entries restricted to State UP.

06

Notes

  • Top 8 ranked players of State in Men/Women/Youth Boys and Girls/ Junior Boys and Girls shall be provided free boarding & lodging (in the hostel). Other players will be provided free accommodation in the hostel if it is booked in advance and is available.
  • Each Institute/Organisation may send one or two teams per event. There is no limit of participants for individual events.
  • The tournament will be played on knock out basis.
  • Each team shall consist of not more than three players.
  • For Women Team Events, there shall be a minimum of two players and maximum 3 players,2 singles and 2 reverse singles with one doubles match will be played. Doubles matches can be played by any of the team members.
  • The tournament will be conducted as per TTFI rules.
  • There will be three players in the team and they will draw for choice of ABC & XYZ playing order & the pattern of play will be as under:
    1. A v/s X
    2. B v/s Y
    3. C v/s Z
    4. B v/s X
  • The decision of the tournament Committee will be final in all the matters.
  • Dress regulation: All the participating players (Except Masters) are requested to wear proper kit like Half Pant, Skirt, T-shirt etc.
