23rd AJAY KUMAR GARG MEMORIAL
TABLE TENNIS TOURNAMENT, 2019

Under the aegis of GATT, Affiliated with UPTTA

05-06 November 2019


About the Tournament

The 23rd annual Ajay Kumar Garg Memorial Table Tennis Tournament is to be conducted on 4-5 November 2017 at Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College in association with GATT, Affiliated with UPTTA. This tournament is state level which is held every year for all professional institutions and schools of the state. Various teams mark their participation in 8 different categories for men and women separately in individual and team events. A huge amount is kept as the prize money.
